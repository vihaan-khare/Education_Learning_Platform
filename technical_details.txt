TECH STACK EXPLAINED
====================

This document explains the "How" and "Why" behind the technology used in this platform.

1. THE BUILDING BLOCKS (Frontend)
---------------------------------
* FRAMEWORK: React 19
  - Why? It lets us build "reusable pieces" of UI. For students with Autism, consistency is key, and React makes it easy to ensure every button and card behaves the same way everywhere.
* BUILD TOOL: Vite
  - Why? Speed. When developers make a change to a button or a color, it updates in the browser instantly (less than 100ms). This allowed us to perfect the "Focus Mode" without wasting time waiting for the app to reload.

2. THE BRAINS (AI & LLMs)
-------------------------
* AI MODEL: Google Gemini 2.0 Flash
  - Why? For neurodivergent users, "Waiting" = "Anxiety." Gemini Flash is the fastest model available. It responds almost instantly, so the student doesn't get distracted while waiting for the AI to clarify a task.
* INSTRUCTION CLARIFIER:
  - What it does: It takes "messy" or "scary" paragraphs of text and turns them into a simple, clear checklist.
* EXPECTATION SETTING:
  - What it does: AI "glances" at the next lesson for you. It tells you exactly how long it will take and what you need to do, which stops the "fear of the unknown" before it starts.

3. PRIVACY FIRST (Onboarding)
-----------------------------
* AI DETECTION: TensorFlow.js
  - Why? Most AI sends your voice or video to a server in the cloud. We don't do that.
  - How? TensorFlow.js lets the AI run "locally" on your computer. Your face/video never leaves your device—it's private by design.

4. SPECIAL "CALM" TOOLS
-----------------------
* FOCUS MODE:
  - Technical Detail: A "one-click" switch that narrows the screen and changes colors to soft, warm tones. This reduces glare and sensory overload.
* PROGRESS MAPPING:
  - Technical Detail: Instead of just saying "Not Done," we calculate exactly how many steps are left. Seeing "Step 3 of 5" gives the user a visible "finish line" which lowers stress.

5. DATA & SYNCING
-----------------
* DATABASE: Firebase
  - Why? It's like a real-time hard drive. If a student finishes a task on their phone, it shows up as "Done" on their computer instantly.
* STYLING: Vanilla CSS
  - Why? We didn't use generic templates. Every pixel is custom-coded to ensure the spacing and colors are perfect for accessibility.
